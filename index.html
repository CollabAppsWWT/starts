One signature covers all three legal agreements.

1. Equity Investment Agreement

You are investing in MidFX AI Pte Ltd in exchange for straight equity (ordinary shares). Your investment confers co-ownership rights and the right to use MidFX FX infrastructure for your own commercial international payment operations. This right flows from ownership, not a service contract. Re-selling FX services to third parties is strictly prohibited. Shares may be transferred with Board approval — MidFX retains right of first refusal. Governed by Singapore law; disputes referred to SIAC arbitration.

2. Limited Scope Agency Agreement

You authorise MidFX AI Pte Ltd to act as your limited-scope agent solely to: (a) open and maintain FX and payment provider accounts with Airwallex, Wise Platform, Currencycloud, and OFX on your behalf; (b) submit your KYC/KYB documentation to those providers; and (c) execute international payment instructions as directed by you. MidFX is explicitly prohibited from transacting your funds except on your direction. Each payment requires your MFA authorisation. You may revoke this authority at any time by written notice.

3. Platform Terms of Use

The platform may only be used for your own company's international commercial payment operations. MidFX screens all payments against international sanctions lists. Every payment requires MFA authorisation. Your KYC documents are stored securely and shared only with nominated providers. Platform access may be suspended immediately upon breach.
